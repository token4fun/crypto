How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move your ship:
- Up Arrow: Accelerate
- Left/Right Arrows: Rotate
- Space Bar: Fire lasers

You start with 3 lives (displayed as mini-ships on the top left).
Your score appears at the top right and the best score (BEST) in the center.
Avoid asteroids and destroy them with your lasers!

About the Game

Cakemoon Asteroids Battle is a modern, neon-infused reimagining of the classic Atari Asteroids game.
Enjoy dynamic glow effects, responsive animations, and a futuristic interface designed for the metaverse.
